Golden Ninetails the Gallant channels the ancient and fierce power of the legendary Nine-Tailed Fox. With a golden aura that radiates energy, they possess incredible strength and speed, making them nearly unstoppable in battle. Known for their fearless nature and sense of justice, Golden Ninetails stands as a protector of peace, using their power for good.

Powers of Golden Ninetails the Gallant:

  • Fox Chakra Cloak
  • Rasengan Mastery
  • Beastly Speed
